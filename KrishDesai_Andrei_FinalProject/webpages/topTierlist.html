Choosing a Champ

The tier list can help you pick your first top lane champ offering you the current meta picks sorted by win-rate.

Below is a mini spotlight of the current best meta pick Malphite. Hover over the ability icons to see what they do.

Granite Shield

Malphite is shielded by a layer of rock which abosorbs damage up to 10% of his maximum health. If Malphite has not been hit for a few seconds, this effect recharges.

Seismic Shard

Malphite sends a shard of the earth through the ground at his foe, dealing damage upon impact and stealing "Move Speed" for 3 seconds

Thunderclap

Malphite attacks with such force that it creates a sonic boom. For the next few seconds, his attacks create aftershocks in front of him.

Ground Slam

Malphite slams the ground, sending out a shockwave that deals magic damage based on his Armor and reduces the Attack Speed of enemies for a short duration.

Unstoppable Force

Malphite launches himself to a location at high speed, damaging enemies and knocking them into the air.

Key Insights

"Thunderclap"'s bonus armor is tripled while "Granite Shield" isn't broken.

"Thunderclap" can also be used as an auto attack reset.

Enemies hit by "Ground Slam" also have their attack speed reduced.

"Unstoppable Force's" speed is increased by Malphite's bonus move speed.

Strengths

Naturally counters AD champions and scales directly with how much Armor he has.

"Seismic Shard" counters champions with movespeed boosts, taking a chunk of the movespeed for himself.

A well-positioned "Unstoppable Force" can singlehandedly win a teamfight.
