Role of the top laner in League of Legends

Top lane has a volatile role on the rift. Their laning phase is often affected by various factors such as:

Jungle Ganks - A good jungle gank can very quickly spin the game in your favor

Other laners teleporting - A well timed teleport could get you killed after a fight

Early game objective fights - Having Lane priority can get you an early objective. The rift herald can make

Champs to Get You Started

Garen

A proud and noble warrior, Garen fights as one of the Dauntless Vanguard. He is popular among his fellows, and respected well enough by his enemies—not least as a scion of the prestigious Crownguard family, entrusted with defending Demacia

Riven

Once a swordmaster in the warhosts of Noxus, Riven is an expatriate in a land she previously tried to conquer. She rose through the ranks on the strength of her conviction and brutal efficiency, and was rewarded with a legendary runic blade

Darius

There is no greater symbol of Noxian might than Darius, the nation's most feared and battle-hardened commander. Rising from humble origins to become the Hand of Noxus, he cleaves through the empire's enemies—many of them Noxians themselves.
